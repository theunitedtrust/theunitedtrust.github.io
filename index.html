About Us

United Trust is a philanthropic organization dedicated to creating a positive impact on society through various charitable and social activities. Founded on the principles of compassion, empathy, and a strong commitment to serve the community, we strive to uplift lives and build a better tomorrow for all.

Our Vision

At United Trust, we envision a world where every individual has access to essential resources, education, and healthcare, irrespective of their socio-economic background. We aim to foster a society that thrives on kindness, equality, and inclusivity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spearhead transformative initiatives that address critical social issues and provide sustainable solutions. Through our diverse range of projects, we aim to foster development, nurture talent, and spread love and hope to those in need.

What We Do

At United Trust, our mission is to touch lives and inspire change through a diverse array of impactful initiatives. From building schools and hospitals to providing food for the hungry, offering affordable housing, and fostering knowledge enrichment, we are dedicated to creating a brighter, more inclusive future for all. Our commitment to education, healthcare, community development, and social welfare is driven by a passion to make a lasting difference and uplift communities, hand in hand, towards a better tomorrow.

Education for All

Empowering the next generation is at the heart of our endeavors. We establish schools and educational centers to provide quality education to children from underprivileged backgrounds, ensuring they have access to a brighter future.

Healthcare for the Vulnerable

Caring for the health and well-being of the less fortunate is paramount. We run hospitals and medical clinics to offer essential healthcare services to those who cannot afford it, giving them a chance to lead healthier lives.

A Space for Community

Our convention center serves as a hub for community gatherings, events, and workshops that promote learning, cultural exchange, and social cohesion.

Affordable Housing

We believe that everyone deserves a safe and secure place to call home. Our flats and housing projects aim to provide affordable and comfortable living spaces for families in need.

Knowledge Enrichment

United Trust is proud to house an international library that caters to knowledge seekers from all walks of life. We are committed to fostering a culture of continuous learning and enlightenment.

Divine Research Center

Our divine research center facilitates the study and preservation of various cultural and spiritual practices, promoting understanding and harmony among diverse communities.

Administrative Support

Efficient administration is crucial for the success of any charitable organization. Our administrative office ensures seamless operations and transparency in all our endeavors.

Celebrating Love

In an effort to make weddings accessible to the less fortunate, we organize marriages for poor individuals and couples, providing them with the joyous celebration they deserve.

Eradicating Hunger

United Trust runs daily food programs that provide nutritious meals to the hungry and impoverished, alleviating hunger and promoting well-being.

Our Volunteers

Our volunteers are the beating heart of United Trust, embodying the spirit of selflessness and compassion. With their dedication and unwavering support, we stand united in our mission to bring hope, joy, and positive change to the lives of those we serve. Together, we create a powerful force for good, making a profound impact on the world, one act of kindness at a time.

Our Message

Dear Brothers and Sisters,

We are delighted to introduce our new charity and humanity project called "United House Hyderabad India."

Our main aim and objective are to promote acts of kindness and compassion within our society in Hyderabad, guided by the principles of Humanity. We believe that every human being possesses a "God Spirit," and it is essential to bridge the gaps that have emerged due to ignorance and misguided religious and social practices leading to superstitions. By doing so, we hope to bring everyone back to the true original divine path.

With this vision in mind, we are committed to establishing a new Divine Empire in Hyderabad, equipped with various facilities to serve the welfare of humanity. Our sincere effort is to nurture the true spirit of understanding, spreading the message of God, and enabling all of us to embark on a journey towards glory, peace, and prosperity.

It is crucial to remember that the essence of our mission lies not only in adhering to the letter of the rules but also in upholding the spirit of our intentions. At United Trust, we extend our hand to guide you through this beautiful journey of self-discovery, from being human to connecting with the divine, ultimately achieving inner peace, prosperity, and oneness with the Supreme.

Join us in this noble endeavor as we strive to create a better world for all.
